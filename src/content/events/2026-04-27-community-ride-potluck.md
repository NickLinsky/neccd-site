One of our favorite events of the year — a casual group ride followed by a community potluck picnic at Peters Park. This is the perfect event to bring friends, family, or anyone curious about cycling with NECCD.

## The ride

We'll do a relaxed 6-mile loop through the South End and Lower Roxbury, exploring some of our favorite streets and greenways. The pace is slow and conversational — if you can talk, you're going the right speed.

This is a **no-drop ride**: we stay together as a group and wait for everyone at turns and lights.

## The potluck

After the ride, we'll gather at Peters Park for a potluck picnic. Please bring a dish to share! Any food is welcome — home cooked, store bought, or from a local bakery. We'll have a few drinks available.

## Details

- **Bikes:** All types welcome — road, mountain, hybrid, cargo, e-bikes
- **Helmets:** Required
- **Loaner bikes:** A limited number are available — email us at least a week in advance
- **Families:** Kids and family bikes are very welcome!

No RSVP required — just show up. If you have questions, join our Band community or email us.
