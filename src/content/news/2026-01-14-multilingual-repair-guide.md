We're excited to announce that our **NECCD Bike Repair Guide** — covering basic maintenance, flat fixes, and gear adjustments — is now available for free download in three languages:

- **English**
- **Spanish** (Español)
- **Haitian Creole** (Kreyòl ayisyen)

## About the guide

The guide was produced entirely by NECCD volunteers and covers the repairs and adjustments most cyclists need to know: fixing a flat tire, adjusting brakes, lubricating a chain, adjusting saddle height, and more. It's written in plain, non-technical language and illustrated with clear diagrams.

The Spanish and Haitian Creole translations were produced by community members who wanted to make sure these resources were accessible to riders in their communities. We're incredibly grateful for their time and skill.

## Download the guide

You can download all three versions for free from our resources page. Print copies will also be available at upcoming NECCD rides — just ask a volunteer.

## Want to help translate?

We'd love to expand the guide to additional languages, including Portuguese, Somali, and Mandarin. If you're interested in helping with a translation, please email info@neccd.bike — no prior translation experience required, just fluency and a willingness to help.

## Bike repair clinics

Later this spring, we'll be hosting a series of in-person bike repair clinics based on the guide. Watch the events calendar for dates and locations.
